Understanding Carbon Footprints

We calculate carbon emissions by considering factors like fuel type, distance, and energy efficiency, using established formulas that comply with Euro Emissions Standards. In a world increasingly focused on environmental sustainability, understanding and mitigating our carbon footprint is essential. Here, we outline the methodologies behind our calculations, providing transparency and insight into how we measure carbon emissions. Our data sources include the Michelin Website, Chalmers University, Vy and Future in Our Hands.

Our app follows a Freemium Model, offering basic features for free and advanced features via subscription. We welcome partnerships to enhance our services and promote sustainable transportation. Subscriptions provide access to premium features like detailed emission reports and personalized recommendations.

Our Methodology

The General Formula:
CO2 = d*(fe/p)
fe: emission factor in CO2/km
d: distance
p: number of passengers
